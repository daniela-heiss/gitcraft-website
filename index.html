What is Gitcraft?

Want to create awesome Projects with your friends ? Design grand buildings and be able to test different variants of your design ? Or just want a handy way of using multiple worlds on your server? Gitcraft is the plugin for you ! Featuring an easy to use way of cloning worlds and merging them back together , a visualizer to see what you are working on and a chat GUI so you don't have to remember 50+ commands, Gitcraft is the solution for server admins and users alike!

We started developing Gitcraft as a university project called "Teamorientiertes Projekt", short "TOP", at the university of applied scienes in Worms, Germany.
